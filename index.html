The Kasadyaan Story

Welcome

Hi wanderers! I am Joana Andrea Subrar, currently studying Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management!

Dating back to December of 1949, the year of its founding, Foundation University began to celebrate this Kasadyaan Festival, formerly called "Mardi Gras." Full of Activties and performances, Foundationites join to celerbate the culture, music, and arts. My friends and I really enjoyed our time both being part of the performances and watching others perform. It was a memorable few days filled with joy and laughter. Please join me as I showcase some of my favorite moments of the Kasadyaan!

DAY 1

Kasadyaan Opening

We join the mass at SSS Hall and went out to see the buglasayaw dance troupe dancing the cultural that has been performed after cutting the ribbon. We explore everything inside specially the booths. We ate and chill then went back to our room for entourage practice. We helped our Pasundayag dancers to prepare everything cause they going to dance in the evening. We went to eat and watch Pasundayag, I was really having fun watching different types of genres in a cultural dance. Our department placed 2nd runner up!

DAY 2

Performance Day

There was a lot of activities and events. Like singing contest, trashionshow, kpop dances and everything. We watched every event cause we are required to watch even doe we gonna do the entourage in the evening. After the events, we stayed in our department suite room to rest and put a make up on for our entourage. We went to our room and prepared everything, prayed for our Hara and also our performance. Then we performed entourage and I was thankful we did it. We watched Hara sa FU until the event ends. We got top 5 in our Hara but sadly we did not get minor and major awards, but atleast we enjoying the day.
